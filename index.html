Each HIT contains a question-response pair from a Qatar social forum. Both question and response are composed of two fields. The question has "short" and a "full" fields and the response has "subject" and "body" fields. Typically, one is a short version of the other, but sometimes they contain complementary information. Your job is to read each question-response pair and judge the appropriateness of the response to the best of your ability. You can select from the following choices as to the nature of the response:

  • Good (e.g., the response answers the question - the information might or might not be correct)
  • Bad (e.g., the response does not answer the question)
  • Potential (e.g., the response provides potentially useful additional information, but does not answer the question directly)
  • Dialogue (e.g. the response posed a question which invites a sub-dialogue or the original poster of the question submitted a comment)
  • Not English (e.g., the response is not in English)
  • Other (e.g., a rare choice for the case no other category is appropriate)

Please study the examples before working on the HIT. Your work will be reviewed by humans, so please do not try to cheat.
